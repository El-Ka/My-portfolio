Hi !

I graduated Wrocław University of Science and Technology. But before that I attended High School of Arts. Those two different schools showed me how amazing is to combain science and art.


As an architect engineer I know how to find solutions to different problems simultaneously. That's why all my websites has one straight idea in all aspects (visual, functional and technical).


Everything I've learn so far about creating websites I learned by myself. Most of my knowledge is based on practice.


When I start design I focuse on user needs such as intuitive interface, clear grahic design or responsive design. I try my best to make my code readable and simple.
